Waste Pattern Detection Agent

  • Food & Beverage / Hospitality

Agent Overview

The Waste Pattern Detection Agent analyzes kitchen prep sheets, waste logs, and optional sales data to identify recurring ingredient waste patterns. It detects over-prep trends, aligns them with demand, and recommends operational changes like portion adjustments or demand-driven prep plans.

 Operational Efficiency
Cost Control

Business Benefits

  • Automated detection of waste patterns reduces manual tracking effort
  • Early identification of inefficiencies enables proactive cost-saving measures
  • Actionable recommendations reduce food costs and optimize prep planning
  • Continuous feedback-driven improvement enhances long-term operational efficiency

Problem Statement

Manual waste tracking fails to catch recurring inefficiencies, leading to high food costs due to over-prep and reactive corrections that don’t address root causes.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI highlights waste trends but doesn’t provide actionable insights. This agent automates waste pattern detection and connects trends with actionable recommendations, enabling proactive cost control and operational efficiency.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Analysis of waste logs, kitchen prep sheets, and sales data
✅ Detection of recurring waste patterns and over-prep trends
✅ Correlation of waste with demand fluctuations
✅ Recommendations for portion adjustments and prep plan optimization

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Daily or weekly updates from waste logs, prep sheets, and sales data.

Agent Evaluation

Identifies patterns in ingredient waste and aligns them with historical demand and sales trends.

Decision Logic

Recommends operational changes like reducing prep portions or aligning schedules with demand forecasts.

Actions Taken

Sends recommendations to kitchen managers and updates waste reduction dashboards.

Escalation / Human Input

Kitchen managers review and implement recommendations, providing feedback for refinement.

Learning / Feedback

Tracks the impact of recommendations to improve detection and suggestion accuracy.

Tools & Components Used

* Domo Workflows
* Integration with kitchen management and inventory systems
* Custom Domo Brick for waste visualization and analysis
* Predictive analytics tools for demand and waste correlation
