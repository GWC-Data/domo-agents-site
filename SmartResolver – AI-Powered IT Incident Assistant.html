SmartResolver – AI-Powered IT Incident Assistant

  • IT Services / Enterprise Support

Agent Overview:

Smart Resolver automatically analyzes new incident tickets, suggests resolution steps using historical ticket data, and assigns the best-fit resolver based on skillsets and availability. Managers receive real-time SLA-based alerts for critical tickets, ensuring accountability and timely resolution.

Business Function: IT Operations & Incident Management

Problem Statement

Manual ticket triage and assignment processes delay resolution. A lack of reference knowledge from past incidents, poor resource assignment, and no real-time SLA monitoring result in inefficiencies, prolonged downtimes, and dissatisfied stakeholders.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI dashboards and workflows focus on tracking and static automation but lack contextual decision-making. SmartResolver uses historical and real-time data to analyze and assign resources dynamically while providing proactive recommendations for faster resolutions.

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Ingestion of new incident tickets from the ITSM system.

Agent Evaluation

SmartResolver analyzes ticket details, maps historical incidents, and evaluates resolver suitability based on skills and availability.

Decision Logic

Determines optimal resolver assignment or escalates high-priority tickets requiring manager oversight.

Actions Taken

Sends resolution recommendations to the assigned resolver or escalates the ticket to the manager for review.

Escalation / Human Input

Managers intervene in case of severe incidents requiring policy overrides or additional approvals.

Learning / Feedback

Logs resolution patterns and outcomes to refine AI decision-making over time.
