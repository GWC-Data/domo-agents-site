SmartResolver – AI-Powered IT Incident Assistant

  • IT Services / Enterprise Support

Automatically analyzes new incident tickets, suggests resolution steps using historical ticket analysis, and auto-assigns best-fit resolver. Managers get SLA-based alerts for critical tickets.

Pain Points before Agents:

Manual ticket triage, delayed resolution due to lack of reference knowledge, poor resource assignment, and no real-time SLA monitoring

Benefits After Agents:

Automated PDF parsing, proactive alerts for renewals/compliance, lease dashboards, and seamless CRM/ERP updates for lease lifecycle tracking
