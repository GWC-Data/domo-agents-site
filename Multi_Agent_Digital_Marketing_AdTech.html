Agent Overview

This multi-agent system automates and optimizes the full lifecycle of digital advertising campaigns—analyzing performance, tracking customer sentiment, detecting risk, and dynamically reallocating budgets. By synchronizing data and decisions across campaign elements, it maximizes effectiveness and reduces wasted spend.

Business Benefits

  • Improved ROI with dynamic ad spend adjustment
  • Early detection of underperforming or risky campaigns
  • 40–60% faster response time to negative sentiment and feedback
  • More effective use of marketing budget based on real impact
  • Enhanced consistency between campaign performance and customer sentiment

Problem Statement

Marketers previously had to juggle campaign analytics, customer feedback, and budgeting decisions manually across fragmented tools. This led to delayed insights, inconsistent messaging, misaligned spending, and lower ROI. Lack of sentiment awareness and campaign risk monitoring further hampered marketing effectiveness.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI platforms show campaign performance but can’t optimize it. ML models may flag sentiment trends but don’t influence spend allocation. Workflows are too static to adapt to evolving customer feedback and performance. This agent brings together campaign analysis, customer sentiment, and intelligent budget orchestration in a fully adaptive loop.

Agent Capabilities

Workflow Explanation

Campaign Analyzer Agent
  • Evaluates campaign performance across KPIs: CTR, ROAS, engagement, conversion.
  • Identifies underperforming and outperforming campaigns.
Feedback Sentiment Analyzer
  • Analyzes customer feedback to compute sentiment scores.
  • Flags high-risk campaigns with negative impact.
  • Predicts future performance based on sentiment history.
Risk Identification
  • Flags risky campaigns needing urgent attention.
  • Highlights need for creative refresh, messaging changes, or budget shifts.
Budget Reallocation Trigger
  • Decides if budget should be rebalanced based on risk and performance.
  • Sends data to Campaign Ads Budget Allocator.
Campaign Ads Budget Allocator
  • Recommends increased budget for high performers.
  • Suggests reducing/pausing underperformers or high-risk campaigns.
  • Flags campaigns with budget risks (negative impact or ROI).
Approval for Budget Reallocation
  • Routes decision to marketing manager or campaign owner.
  • If Approved: Reallocates spend across campaigns.
  • If Rejected: Ends workflow and logs the recommendation.
End
  • Campaign actions are executed or archived based on approval outcome.
