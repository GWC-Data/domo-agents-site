Agent Overview

The AI-Driven Inventory Disposal Agent automates the decision-making process around aging or non-moving inventory. It evaluates each inventory unit for spoilage risk, carrying cost, and operational viability, and recommends the most cost-effective action: direct disposal, markdown sale, or inter-warehouse transfer. This ensures inventory is aligned with demand, reduces waste, and improves warehouse utilization.

Problem Statement

Inventory teams traditionally rely on static rules or lagging metrics like sales velocity to flag aging stock. While ML may forecast spoilage, it does not support nuanced trade-off analysis or provide actionable disposal/redistribution recommendations. Most decisions are manual and lack integration with procurement or warehouse operations, leading to lost margins and inefficiencies.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI dashboards highlight stagnant inventory but don't recommend optimal actions. ML may predict obsolescence but doesn’t provide financial trade-offs between markdown, disposal, or transfer. Traditional workflows lack dynamic evaluation of storage costs, warehouse capacity, or promotional pricing effectiveness. This agent merges ML insights with operational simulation to guide inventory treatment decisions.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Detects low-velocity or obsolete inventory
✅ Simulates disposal, markdown, and transfer options
✅ Recommends action with financial and operational trade-off context

Workflow Explanation

Inventory Disposal Catalyst
  • Initiates the workflow and classifies inventory into potential actions:
    • Dispose
    • Mark Down
    • Transfer
Dispose Path
  • Identifies units unsuitable for use and recommends direct disposal.
Mark Down Path
  • Suggests discounted sales through promotions for viable SKUs.
Transfer Path
  • Recommends transfer of units to another location with demand
    • e.g., local to central warehouse
Resultant Table
  • Logs the selected action and resulting inventory adjustments.
Inventory Shifted (if applicable)
  • Tracks inter-warehouse transfers and closes loop on inventory movement.
