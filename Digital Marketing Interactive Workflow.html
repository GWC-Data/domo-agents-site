Agent Overview

The Digital Marketing Interactive Workflow agent provides a guided, AI-powered process to plan, test, validate, and launch digital campaigns. It centralizes the steps into a single flow—from audience targeting to creative testing and performance simulation—accelerating campaign readiness and ensuring consistency across platforms and teams.

Business Benefits

  • 40–60% faster campaign planning cycles
  • Improved cross-team coordination and visibility
  • Smarter spend with predictive simulation of campaign ROI
  • Higher consistency and quality in GTM execution
  • Reduced manual effort across campaign lifecycle

Problem Statement

Traditional campaign planning was fragmented across multiple tools and teams—each handling targeting, creatives, copy, and testing separately. Manual coordination, versioning issues, and the lack of standardized workflows slowed campaign execution and made it hard to scale. Testing ideas or projecting outcomes was either skipped or relied on guesswork.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

  • BI tools provide historical insights but don’t support interactive planning.
  • ML models may predict outcomes but don’t guide campaign assembly.
  • Workflow tools aren’t tailored to simulate campaign impact or tie actions together contextually.

This agent combines structured campaign logic, behavioral simulation, and strategic prompts to deliver a self-serve, intelligent GTM planning environment.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Guides users through a step-by-step digital campaign planning journey
✅ Suggests:
  • Best audience segments based on past engagement
  • Channel mix and content formats
  • Campaign budget ranges and scheduling windows
✅ Simulates expected performance (CTR, ROAS, conversions)
✅ Auto-generates campaign briefs and creatives for review
✅ Tracks testing status, stakeholder approvals, and final deployment

Workflow Explanation

Campaign Objective Selector
  • Marketer selects primary goal:
    • Awareness
    • Engagement
    • Conversion
    • Retargeting
Audience & Channel Recommender
  • Agent suggests:
    • Target segments (based on past behavior)
    • Optimal channels (Google, Meta, Email, LinkedIn)
    • Campaign duration and timing
Creative Concept Generator
  • Prompts for:
    • Product focus
    • USP (Unique Selling Proposition)
    • Tone of voice
  • Generates copy and creative variations for A/B testing
Budget & Performance Simulator
  • Agent Simulates:
    • Click-through rate
    • ROAS
    • Conversion rate
    • Estimated reach per channel
  • Offers trade-off insights: “Spend X more for 15% more reach”
Campaign Brief Generator
  • Generates a structured campaign brief including:
    • Objectives
    • Targeting
    • Budget
    • Creatives
    • Approval status
Testing & Feedback Tracker
  • Tracks:
    • Tested versions
    • Feedback received
    • Approved creatives
  • Updates campaign dashboard for GTM team
Launch Ready
  • Once approved, agent triggers launch via ad platform integration or hands off final kit to ops team
  • Logs all versions and final decisions

Tools & Components Used

* MagicETL: For historical campaign data analysis
* Agent Prompts: Copy generation, simulation insights, recommendation logic
* Domo Workflows: For approval tracking and task routing
