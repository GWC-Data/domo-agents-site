Dynamic Menu Optimization Agent

  • Food & Beverage / QSR / Hospitality

Agent Overview

The Dynamic Menu Optimization Agent analyzes real-time sales trends, inventory levels, weather forecasts, and predicted customer footfall to recommend optimized daily menu items. It automates menu planning and suggests changes to chefs or branch managers for maximum efficiency and profitability.

 Menu Planning
Operational Efficiency

Business Benefits

  • Data-driven menu recommendations reduce wastage and optimize inventory usage
  • Increased sales through dynamic and context-aware menu adjustments
  • Improved operational efficiency with automated menu planning
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction by offering relevant and in-demand items

Problem Statement

Manual menu planning struggles to adapt to dynamic demand patterns, leading to high food wastage, missed revenue opportunities during peak times, and inefficiencies in aligning inventory with sales trends.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI dashboards provide historical data, while workflows handle repetitive tasks. This agent goes beyond by reasoning dynamically with contextual data to suggest actionable, demand-driven menu adjustments in real time.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Real-time analysis of sales trends and inventory levels
✅ Weather and customer footfall impact assessment
✅ Automated menu optimization recommendations
✅ Communication of daily menu adjustments to chefs or managers

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Daily updates from sales systems, inventory reports, and weather forecasts.

Agent Evaluation

Analyzes data to identify high-performing items, surplus inventory, and demand influencers.

Decision Logic

Generates menu recommendations aligned with expected demand and operational constraints.

Actions Taken

Sends optimized menu suggestions to chefs or branch managers and updates menu planning dashboards.

Escalation / Human Input

Chefs or managers review and approve recommendations, providing feedback if necessary.

Learning / Feedback

Tracks menu performance to refine future recommendations.

Tools & Components Used

* Domo Workflows
* Integration with inventory and sales systems
* Custom Domo Brick for menu visualization and planning
* Weather and footfall data APIs
