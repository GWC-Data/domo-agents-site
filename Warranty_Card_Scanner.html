Warranty Card Scanner

  • E-Commerce / Appliances / Automotive / Retail

Agent Overview

The Warranty Card Scanner automates the extraction of warranty claim details from scanned or uploaded physical warranty cards. It parses key details like product name, serial number, purchase date, issue description, and customer information, ensuring efficient claim processing.

  Warranty Claims Processing
CRM Integration

Business Benefits

  • Automates data extraction, reducing manual effort and errors
  • Speeds up claim processing for improved customer experience
  • Flags expired or invalid claims, ensuring policy adherence
  • Integrates clean and validated data into CRM for seamless support tracking

Problem Statement

Manual entry of warranty card data is error-prone and time-consuming, often leading to misfiled claims, delays in processing, and a lack of seamless CRM integration.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI dashboards provide visibility into claims but can’t automate the extraction or validation process. This agent ensures accurate data capture, validation, and integration into downstream systems.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Automated extraction of warranty details from scanned documents
✅ Validation of claims against warranty policies (e.g., expiry, eligibility)
✅ Auto-logging of valid claims into CRM or support queues
✅ Flagging of invalid or expired claims for further review

  • Tools & Components Used

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Upload or scan of physical warranty cards into the system.

Agent Evaluation

Extracts key details using OCR and validates them against warranty policies.

Decision Logic

Differentiates between valid and invalid claims based on policy checks.

Actions Taken

Logs valid claims into CRM/support queues and flags anomalies for manual review.

Escalation / Human Input

Support teams review flagged claims and handle exceptions.

Learning / Feedback

Tracks common claim issues to refine validation logic and improve processing.

Tools & Components Used

* OCR tools for data extraction from scanned documents
* Domo Workflows for claim validation and routing
* Custom Domo Brick for warranty claim visualization
* Integration with CRM and support systems
