D2C Upsell/Cross-sell Agent

  • Marketing (Direct-to-Consumer)

Agent Overview

The D2C Upsell/Cross-sell Agent intelligently analyzes customer purchase history, behavior patterns, and bundle performance to recommend personalized upsell or cross-sell bundles. It automates both the optimization of existing bundles and the discovery of new ones, increasing relevance and conversion by leveraging real-time behavioral signals.

 Personalized Product Bundling
Revenue Optimization

Business Benefits

  • Increased Average Order Value (AOV) via tailored offers
  • 40–60% improvement in bundle conversion rates
  • More relevant product recommendations based on real behavior
  • Reduced reliance on manual segmentation or static rules
  • Continuous learning and improvement in upsell strategies

Problem Statement

Before implementing this agent, marketing teams had limited insight into the dynamic nature of customer preferences. Relying on manual segmentation or static upsell rules often led to irrelevant offers, resulting in low conversion rates and missed opportunities for increasing Average Order Value (AOV).

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI dashboards show product performance but don’t suggest new bundles or measure upsell potential. ML models may recommend items based on similarity, but without human context or bundle performance tracking. Workflows lack adaptability to changing consumer trends. This agent continuously learns from customer behavior to suggest, refine, and optimize bundles in real time.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Analyzes historical purchase behavior
✅ Suggests new product bundles tailored to segments
✅ Optimizes existing bundles for better performance
✅ Tracks new bundle effectiveness and customer response
✅ Feeds results to campaign systems for targeting
✅ Dynamically updates based on new behavioral signals

Workflow Explanation

Left Branch – Bundle Optimizer Agent
  • Evaluates current product bundles
  • Recommends optimizations based on performance and relevance
  • Produces New Renewed Bundles Data
Right Branch – Bundle Discovery Agent
  • Suggests new product bundles based on customer purchase history
  • Outputs New Bundle Data
  • Monitors and reports how new bundles perform over time

Tools & Components Used

* MagicETL (for behavioral segmentation and bundle construction)
* Agent Prompts (for real-time recommendation generation)
* Domo Workflows
* Integration with eCommerce platforms (e.g., Shopify, BigCommerce APIs)
* Custom Visual Brick for Bundle Performance
