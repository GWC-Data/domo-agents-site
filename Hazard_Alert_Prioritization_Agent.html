Hazard Alert Prioritization Agent

  • Manufacturing

Agent Overview

The Hazard Alert Prioritization Agent ingests sensor data, historical hazard patterns, and real-time contextual information (e.g., location, time, proximity to personnel or high-risk zones) to evaluate and rank hazard alerts by severity. It escalates high-priority risks, enables timely intervention, and streamlines safety workflows.

Business Benefits

  • Accelerates response to genuinely high-risk hazards
  • Reduces alert fatigue by filtering noise
  • Increases safety team efficiency and focus
  • Ensures compliance with audit-ready logs and response histories
  • Protects workers and assets with smarter, real-time decision-making

Problem Statement

Manufacturing environments generate large volumes of sensor-triggered safety alerts. Without prioritization, safety teams are forced to manually sift through them, often treating all hazards as equal. This slows response to critical incidents and increases the likelihood of near misses or delayed containment—particularly in hazardous zones or during peak operations.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

  • BI dashboards show hazard trends but not real-time risk prioritization.
  • ML models may detect anomalies but don’t provide triaged escalation.
  • Standard workflows lack spatial, operational, and historical context for urgency scoring.

This agent uses intelligent alert prioritization that integrates operational context to ensure the right teams focus on the right risks at the right time.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Monitors live hazard alerts (e.g., gas leaks, temperature spikes, pressure drops)
✅ Evaluates alerts based on:
  • Historical severity
  • Time of day and staffing level
  • Proximity to workers, high-risk areas, or sensitive machinery
  • Cumulative or recurring patterns
✅ Assigns a Hazard Priority Score
✅ Automatically escalates top-tier risks to EHS/safety teams
✅ Logs all alerts, actions taken, and outcomes for auditability

Workflow Explanation

Alert Intake & Contextualization
  • Ingests real-time sensor alert with metadata:
    • Alert type (chemical, electrical, thermal, etc.)
    • Time of occurrence
    • Location coordinates
    • Nearby personnel or equipment tags
    • Repetition frequency
Risk Scoring Engine
  • Calculates risk score using:
    • Hazard type severity
    • Location risk classification
    • Worker proximity (based on shift rosters or tracking)
    • Historical incident similarity or recurrence
    • Time sensitivity (e.g., shift change, lone worker hours)
Alert Prioritization & Escalation
  • Categorizes alert as:
    • High-Risk (Critical): Immediate escalation
    • Medium-Risk: Log and notify team lead
    • Low-Risk: Log only unless repeated
  • Escalates critical alerts to:
    • Safety operations dashboard
    • Email/SMS/PA system (if configured)
    • Incident ticketing system
Action Logging & Feedback
  • Captures response action:
    • Acknowledged
    • Resolved
    • False alarm
  • Feedback used to refine future prioritization logic
Summary Output
  • Updates alert log dataset
  • Triggers automated daily safety report if threshold breached
