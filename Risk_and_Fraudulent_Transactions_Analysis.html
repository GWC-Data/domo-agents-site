Agent Overview

The Risk and Fraudulent Transactions Analysis Agent is a composite system made up of intelligent sub-agents that monitor transaction patterns to identify fraud, liquidity risks, and terminal anomalies. It not only predicts fraudulent behavior but also provides automated notifications and decision workflows, enabling faster response and reducing financial impact.

Problem Statement

Traditional systems for fraud and risk management are siloed and reactive. They depend on static rules or batch reviews that miss evolving fraud tactics and create overwhelming false positives. Analysts and managers are burdened with manual approvals, delaying action and increasing risk exposure.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI shows what happened but not what to do next. ML models might detect fraud but often work in isolation without escalation or decision routing. Workflows lack coordination across fraud, terminal, and liquidity assessments. This agent combines ML-based detection, approval logic, and multi-team escalation into a single intelligent workflow.

Workflow Explanation

Fraud Behavior Intelligence Agent
  • Analyzes historical and current transactions to predict fraudulent activity.
  • Sends an alert to the Fraud Team for review.
Fraud Team Decision
  • Team chooses to Approve or Deny the transaction.
  • If Approved: The workflow ends.
  • If Denied: Notification sent to Security Team for investigation, then ends.
Customer Liquidity Risk Predictor
  • Assesses customer’s financial risk and predicts potential liquidity issues.
  • Sends alert to the Manager for review.
Manager Decision
  • Manager decides whether to Contact the Customer based on risk assessment.
  • If Yes: Notification is sent to the customer.
  • If No: Ends without action.
Terminal Risk Evaluator
  • Monitors transaction patterns from various terminals to detect abnormal activity.
  • Sends alert to the Terminal Team.
Terminal Team Decision
  • Evaluates flagged terminal and chooses to Approve or Deny.
  • If Approved: The workflow ends.
  • If Denied: A security alert is sent to the Security Team, and the workflow ends.
