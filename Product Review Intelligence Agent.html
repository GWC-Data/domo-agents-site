Product Review Intelligence Agent

  • E-Commerce / Consumer Electronics / Retail

Agent Overview

The Product Review Intelligence Agent continuously monitors customer reviews to detect sentiment trends, identify recurring product issues, and cluster feedback themes. It summarizes top praises and pain points, delivering actionable alerts to product teams for timely improvements.

Customer Feedback Analysis
Product Development

Business Benefits

  • Early detection of emerging product issues reduces warranty claims and returns
  • Actionable AI-prioritized insights improve product roadmaps
  • Timely response to feedback enhances customer satisfaction and loyalty
  • Reduced manual effort in review analysis allows teams to focus on strategic decisions

Problem Statement

Manual review of large volumes of customer feedback is time-consuming and often misses emerging product issues. This delay in reacting to declining sentiment impacts product quality and customer satisfaction.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI tools surface aggregate sentiment but lack proactive issue detection and actionable insights. This agent uses AI to cluster feedback, highlight critical themes, and prioritize actions, enabling product teams to respond proactively.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Sentiment analysis of product reviews
✅ Clustering of recurring feedback themes
✅ Detection of emerging product issues and customer concerns
✅ Summarization of praises and pain points for actionable insights

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Periodic ingestion of product reviews from platforms like e-commerce sites or feedback tools.

Agent Evaluation

Analyzes sentiment trends and clusters reviews to identify recurring issues and key praises.

Decision Logic

Prioritizes feedback themes and determines actionable alerts for product teams.

Actions Taken

Sends summaries of insights and critical alerts to product teams, with recommendations for improvement.

Escalation / Human Input

Product teams review critical alerts for validation and act on recommended changes.

Learning / Feedback

Tracks outcomes of alerts and feedback resolutions to refine detection and recommendations.

Tools & Components Used

* Sentiment analysis and natural language processing tools
* Domo Workflows for alert routing and visualization
* Integration with review platforms and feedback tools
* Custom Domo Brick for trend and theme visualization
