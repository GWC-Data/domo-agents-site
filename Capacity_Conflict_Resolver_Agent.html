Agent Overview

The Capacity Conflict Resolver Agent dynamically analyzes shift schedules, job orders, labor availability, and machine capacity to detect potential conflicts or bottlenecks in production. It evaluates trade-offs in real time and recommends adjustments—such as job reshuffling, overtime use, or alternate routing—to ensure smooth, efficient operations.

Business Benefits

  • Reduces production delays by resolving conflicts proactively
  • Increases resource utilization (labor, machine, material)
  • Improves on-time order fulfillment
  • Reduces manual firefighting and decision fatigue
  • Standardizes and accelerates conflict resolution with explainable logic

Problem Statement

Although BI tools and ML models can forecast capacity overloads or underutilization, the actual process of resolving these issues is manual. Planners have to balance multiple competing constraints—machine load, shift capacity, labor skill, and inventory—often under pressure and without visibility into the best options. This leads to suboptimal or inconsistent decisions, delayed responses, and missed SLAs.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

  • BI flags the problem but doesn’t solve it.
  • ML forecasts future capacity issues but lacks context on shop floor constraints.
  • Workflows don't weigh trade-offs or provide rationale for alternatives.

This agent combines constraint-aware logic, real-time data feeds, and explainable optimization to recommend the best possible adjustments with execution-ready instructions.

Agent Capabilities

Workflow Explanation

Capacity Snapshot Builder
  • Pulls current:
    • Job queue and order details
    • Shift schedules and labor rosters
    • Machine availability and planned downtime
    • Material inventory status
Conflict Detection Engine
  • Scans for:
    • Double-booked machines
    • Overlapping tasks on same line
    • Unassigned labor due to shift gaps
    • Inventory shortages for scheduled runs
  • Flags issues with timestamps and locations
Optimization & Resolution Recommender
  • Evaluates resolution scenarios using constraint satisfaction:
    • Job A → move to later shift
    • Job B → reroute to Line 3
    • Shift X → assign overtime labor
    • Recommend alternate raw material if stock is low
  • Ranks scenarios by impact on:
    • Throughput
    • On-time delivery
    • Labor efficiency
    • Cost
Decision Justifier & Alert Agent
  • Prepares rationale for top recommendation:
    “Reroute Job #457 to Machine M3 (75% load) to avoid conflict with Job #453. Preserves delivery date and avoids overtime.”
  • Sends alerts to production planner or line manager
  • Option to auto-approve or request manual override
System Sync & Log
  • Syncs approved plan with MES, ERP, or production scheduling tool
  • Logs conflict details, resolution chosen, and final outcome

Tools & Components Used

* MagicETL: Joins job orders, shift plans, inventory, and machine data
* Agent Prompts: For optimization reasoning and trade-off communication
* Domo Workflows: Conflict detection and notification
* Integration with MES/ERP (e.g., SAP PP, Oracle SCM, PLEX)
* Scheduling Visual Brick for real-time capacity visualization
