Lease Agreement Extraction Agent

  • Real Estate / Document Management

Agent Overview

The Lease Agreement Extraction Agent automates the extraction of structured data from lease PDFs and images. It validates lease details, triggers compliance alerts, and populates lease dashboards while seamlessly integrating with CRM/ERP systems for streamlined lease lifecycle management.

Business Benefits

· Automated PDF parsing minimizes manual effort and errors
· Proactive alerts for renewals and compliance reduce risks
· Centralized lease dashboards enhance decision-making with real-time insights
· Seamless updates to CRM/ERP ensure effective lease lifecycle tracking

Problem Statement

Manual data entry of lease agreements is time-consuming and prone to errors. Delayed alerts for expiring leases and missed compliance clauses increase risks. The absence of centralized lease insights hinders effective lease management.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

Traditional BI and workflows rely on manually entered data and predefined rules, lacking the ability to process unstructured documents dynamically. The agent uses AI to extract, validate, and monitor lease details in real time, reducing errors and improving compliance.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Automated extraction of structured data from lease PDFs and images
✅ Validation of lease terms and details for accuracy and compliance
✅ Centralized lease dashboards for actionable insights
✅ AI-guided resolution steps and recommendations
✅ Seamless integration with CRM/ERP systems for lifecycle tracking

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Upload or ingestion of lease documents (PDFs/images) into the system.

Agent Evaluation

Extracts key details such as tenant name, lease term, rent amount, and compliance clauses using AI-powered parsing.

Decision Logic

Validates extracted data against predefined standards and flags discrepancies or upcoming lease expirations.

Actions Taken

Updates lease dashboards with structured data, triggers alerts for compliance or renewals, and syncs information with CRM/ERP.

Escalation / Human Input

Alerts managers for manual review of flagged compliance issues or unresolved extraction errors.

Learning / Feedback

Improves data extraction accuracy by learning from corrections and validation feedback.

Tools & Components Used

* Domo Workflows
* AI-based Document Parser (e.g., OCR with NLP capabilities)
* Custom Domo Brick for lease lifecycle visualization
* CRM/ERP Integration (e.g., Salesforce, SAP)
