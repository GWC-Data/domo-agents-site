Budget Allocation Agent

  • Marketing

Agent Overview

The Budget Allocation Agent intelligently evaluates marketing campaign performance and proposes optimal budget reallocations. By analyzing ROI and ROAS metrics, it identifies top-performing campaigns, estimates the impact of proposed changes, and generates strategic justifications—streamlining approval and enabling smarter, faster budget decisions.

Campaign Budget Optimization
Performance Management

Business Benefits

  • Higher marketing efficiency by shifting funds to proven campaigns
  • Improved ROI with transparent, explainable budget logic
  • Faster budget decisions supported by real-time performance data
  • Reduced waste from overfunding poor performers
  • Clear documentation and traceability for every budget shift

Problem Statement

Previously, budget decisions were based on outdated trends or manager intuition, often leading to inefficient use of marketing spend. Campaigns with strong ROI were underfunded while weaker performers continued receiving budget. There was little visibility into the potential impact of reallocation.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

BI shows campaign metrics but can’t recommend budget moves. ML may predict performance but lacks transparency and explainability. Workflows alone don’t evaluate or justify reallocation. This agent combines smart analytics, impact simulation, and justification into one automated, explainable cycle.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Filters and selects high-performing campaigns based on ROI/ROAS
✅ Estimates ROI impact of shifting budgets
✅ Recommends optimal reallocation across campaigns
✅ Generates executive-level justifications for decisions
✅ Appends reallocation plans to the dataset
✅ Notifies the marketing manager with summary and reasoning

Workflow Explanation

Get Executor
  • Identifies the responsible owner/manager to approve or receive reallocation suggestions.
Budget Allocation Agent
  • Evaluates ROI and ROAS across all active campaigns.
  • Filters valid campaigns (active, not paused, minimum data volume).
  • Selects Top 10 High-Performing Campaigns based on performance metrics.
Estimate ROI Impact with Budget Changes
  • Simulates potential ROI improvement by reallocating budget from low- to high-performing campaigns.
Propose Budget Reallocations
  • Recommends specific budget increases/decreases based on optimization logic.
Generate Strategic Justifications
  • Creates plain-language justifications for each recommendation.
  • Example: “Campaign X consistently outperforms benchmark with 2.8x ROI.”
Parallel Actions
  • Append to Dataset: Logs reallocation plan, justification, and decision owner.
  • Mail Agent: Sends summary notification to the designated marketing manager.
End
  • Waits for manager feedback or auto-executes the reallocation.

Tools & Components Used

* MagicETL (campaign performance metrics, reallocation calculations)
* Agent Prompts (justification generation and top campaign selection)
* Domo Workflows
* Domo Workflows: Automation of decisions and notifications
