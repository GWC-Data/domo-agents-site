Lead Distribution Agent

  • IT Services / Sales / Marketing

Agent Overview

The Lead Distribution Agent automates lead assignment by matching leads with the most suitable sales representative based on region and language. It ensures faster response times and better alignment while seamlessly updating CRM systems or notifying reps directly.

Assignment

Business Benefits

· Intelligent lead assignment ensures alignment with the best-fit sales rep
· Faster response times improve lead engagement and conversion rates
· Automated routing reduces manual workload and errors
· Seamless CRM updates enhance team collaboration and tracking

Problem Statement

Manual lead routing often misaligns leads with the right representatives, causing delays in response and ineffective communication, especially for region- or language-specific leads.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

Traditional BI dashboards and workflows are limited to static assignment rules and can’t dynamically evaluate lead attributes or rep suitability. This agent intelligently assigns leads based on context, ensuring optimal rep alignment and response efficiency.

Agent Capabilities

✅ Automatic lead assignment to sales representatives based on region and language match
✅ Real-time lookup and matching using the Sales_Reps table
✅ Seamless updates to CRM systems with assigned reps
✅ Notifications to sales reps for newly assigned leads

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

New lead creation in the CRM or manual lead upload.

Agent Evaluation

Matches lead attributes (e.g., region, language) with sales reps using the Sales_Reps table.

Decision Logic

Identifies the most suitable sales rep based on criteria like proximity, language match, and workload.

Actions Taken

Assigns the lead to the selected rep, updates CRM records, and notifies the rep of the assignment.

Escalation / Human Input

Sales managers can manually reassign leads in case of special requirements or conflicts.

Learning / Feedback

Refines assignment logic based on feedback and historical data trends.

Tools & Components Used

* Domo Workflows
* Lookup tools for region and language matching
* Custom Domo Brick for lead routing visualization
* CRM Integration (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot)
