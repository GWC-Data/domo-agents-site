Competitor Pitch Selector

  • IT Services / Sales / Marketing

Agent Overview

The Competitor Pitch Selector identifies competitors in deals and uses AI to classify their strengths. It recommends internal pitch decks tagged against these competitors and generates key differentiation points, empowering sales teams with tailored competitive strategies.

Competitive Intelligence
Sales Enablement

Business Benefits

· Timely and competitor-specific pitch support enhances deal strategy
· Sharper value positioning increases sales team confidence and effectiveness
· Reduced reliance on generic pitches improves engagement and win rates
· Continuous refinement of competitive strategies through feedback loops

Problem Statement

Sales teams often lack real-time competitive insights, relying on generic pitches that fail to address specific competitor strengths. This leads to missed opportunities for effective positioning and reduced success in competitive deals.

Why Not Just BI / ML / Workflow?

Traditional BI and workflows lack the ability to dynamically classify competitors and recommend tailored strategies. This agent provides real-time, context-aware support, ensuring competitive advantages are leveraged effectively.

Agent Capabilities

✅ AI classification of competitor strengths and weaknesses based on deal context
✅ Recommendation of pitch decks specifically tagged for identified competitors
✅ Automated generation of differentiation points to position against competitors
✅ Real-time delivery of competitor-specific insights to sales reps
  • Workflow
  • Tools & Components Used

Workflow Explanation

Trigger

Competitor identification during lead qualification or deal stage updates in CRM.

Agent Evaluation

Uses AI to analyze competitor data and classify strengths based on market and deal context.

Decision Logic

Matches competitors with internal pitch decks tagged for relevant positioning and generates differentiation points.

Actions Taken

Delivers tailored recommendations and insights to the assigned sales rep via Buzz or Email.

Escalation / Human Input

Sales teams can validate insights and provide feedback for refining competitor strategies.

Learning / Feedback

Updates competitor profiles and refines recommendations based on deal outcomes and user feedback.

Tools & Components Used

* Agent Catalyst for competitor classification and analysis
* Domo Workflows
* Content repository integration for competitor-tagged pitch decks
* Buzz/Email API for delivering recommendations and insights
